About KiwiLearn

Welcome to KiwiLearn – your child's new favorite learning platform! We are excited to provide a fun, interactive, and curriculum-aligned learning experience for all New Zealand kids.

We understand that learning doesn't just happen in the classroom and that's why we are here to support your child's educational journey outside of school. Our activities are designed to make learning enjoyable, engaging, and effective. From math to science, English to social studies, we have a wide range of activities that will keep your child entertained while they learn.

But it's not just about the kids, teachers and parents play a crucial role in a child's education and that's why we have included tools for both of you. Our platform allows teachers to easily assign and track activities for their students, saving them time and effort. Parents, you can also monitor your child's progress and offer support and encouragement along the way.

Activities

Quizzes

Our quizzes are a great way for kids to test their knowledge on a particular topic. They are interactive, with colorful images and easy-to-understand questions. Plus, with immediate feedback, kids can track their progress and see where they need to focus more.

Games

Learning should be fun and that's why KiwiLearn offers a variety of educational games that are both entertaining and educational. These games cover different subjects and are a great way to make learning enjoyable.

Printable Worksheets

Download and print worksheets for offline practice. Perfect for reinforcing learning at home or in the classroom.

Progress Tracking

Students, parents, and teachers can easily track progress and celebrate achievements with our visual dashboards and points system.

Features

  • Activity Assignment: Assign fun, curriculum-aligned activities to students or children with just a few clicks.
  • Student Activity Tracking: Monitor progress and celebrate achievements with easy-to-read dashboards.
  • Quizzes: Interactive quizzes help reinforce learning and boost confidence.
  • Games: Educational games make learning exciting and memorable.
  • Printable Worksheets: Download and print worksheets for offline practice.
  • User Accounts: Personalized accounts for students, parents, and teachers.
  • Progress Dashboards: Visualize learning journeys and milestones.
  • NZ Curriculum Alignment: All activities are designed to match the New Zealand curriculum.
